FAQ

Got questions? We’ve got answers.

Who should become part of the Comfort Care community?

Our doors are open to senior living Michigan residents who are 55 or above and require special attention. The home-like environment is supported by on-site RNs, assistants, medical specialists, and caring residents.

Am I allowed to meet my loved ones at Comfort Care?

Yes. Family meet-ups are encouraged within appropriate hours so routines stay consistent. A certified assistant can be present when helpful for comfort and peace of mind.

What services are offered?

Comfort Care offers personalized nutrition, healthcare, therapies, entertainment, and indoor and outdoor recreational activities for residents.
